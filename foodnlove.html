FOOD & lOVE

Building Connection between Parents & Children through Food

Starting from the idea to solve problems for internation students concerning their hometown food, the project intended to find themes and patterns of problems they encountered through contextual interviews and observations. By involving other stakeholders, mainly their parents, we began to discover the emotional connections behind international students, their parents, and also food.
