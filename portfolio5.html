DQ Pro

A training program to raise the digital quotient skills of educators and corporate employees.

The goal of DQ Pro is to empower employees around the globe so that they gain the necessary knowledge, skills and abilities to deal with the challenges and demands of the digital era at work, and away from it. By being responsible technology users, they will establish a strong identity and positive values towards their companies and themselves.
DQ Pro is composed of eight curriculum categories, or modules. Each module is approximately one hour and is composed of four different learning sections of 15 minutes. The content will be delivered as a mobile app and learners will be able to work at their own pace.
