Website Re-design

A Usability Report on Museum Websites

In this report, the two websites for usability testing were chosen because the both museums are pretty popular nationwide and both have a pretty high review online. They provide an abundant collection of aircraft and spacecraft and cover a wide range of topics on the development in space and air through the history. Their websites also list these collections and fascinating events for the public to visit. Based on the 3 I's of design, the testing on three participants helped to figure out changes can be done to make the website better.
