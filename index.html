About me

"Be the change you wish to see in the world."

I am in the doctoral program in Learning Technologies at the University of Texas at Austin. Before coming to UT, I studied Digital Media and Education at the University of Michigan-Ann Arbor and graduated in December 2015. My interests in educational technology include interface design, game-based learning, and online learning. I have been working as a web developer on campus for more than 2 years. I love animals and have a cat and a puppy at home and enjoy hiking and swimming.
